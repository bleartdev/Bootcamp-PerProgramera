Welcome to Bakery

Since 2000, Premium Bakery has grown from a small family-run flour company into a leading producer of high-quality bakery and confectionery products in Kosovo,
renowned for our state-of-the-art 12,000-square-meter facility and diverse range of breads and croissants.










Premium Bakery history








HISTORY SINCE 2000

For over 20 years, we’ve blended family values with modern innovation to create exceptional bakery
products. What began as a small flour production business in 2000 has evolved into a regional
leader, known for crafting a wide variety of breads and pastries with care, precision, and
unwavering commitment to quality.
